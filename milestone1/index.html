Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

The site will contain a resume and relevant information about my professional background.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

Most content, including images, will be sourced from my personal portfolio and past job experiences.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

This site provides insight into my skills, experiences, and qualifications, making it easier for recruiters to evaluate my suitability for roles.

Why should a site be built for this?

To create a centralized platform that showcases my professional accomplishments and facilitates connections with job recruiters.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to explore my resume and provide feedback or reach out for opportunities.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal is to provide a comprehensive view of my professional journey and enable potential employers to contact me directly.
